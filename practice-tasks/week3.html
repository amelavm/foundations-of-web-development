Fitness Tips

  1. Warm-up before exercise

    Spend 5–10 minutes warming up your muscles to prevent injury.

  2. Stay hydrated

    Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout to stay hydrated.

  3. Eat a balanced diet

    Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, and proteins in your meals to support your fitness goals.
