Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a CV using the tool?

To create a CV, simply sign up or log in, choose a template, and fill in your personal information, education, skills, and experience. Once done, you can download your CV in various formats.

Can I edit my CV after saving it?

Yes, you can always log in to your account and make edits to your CV whenever needed. All your information is saved securely in your profile.

What file formats are available for download?

You can download your CV in PDF, Word, or plain text formats. Simply select the desired format after completing your CV.

Is there a fee for using the tool?

The tool is free to use for basic CV creation. However, there are premium templates and additional features available for a small fee.
